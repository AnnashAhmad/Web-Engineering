    • Controller of Exams Office

    Controller of Examinations (CoE) is one of the most important departments at CUI. The role of the CoE department, in view of its team, is not of a policeman but it is a “Security Guard” who ensures that any “Degree or transcript” which goes out of this institute is genuine and is being issued to a person who fulfills all the legal requirement for this purpose.
